Big Chocolate

Big Chocolate is the production alias & multi genre music project of Cameron Argon

Are you tired of the same old, over-the-top dubstep that's too loud, too aggressive, and too random? Introducing Dubstep for Moms! Our music is easy to listen to, accessible, and repetitive, so you can enjoy it without feeling overwhelmed. Plus, it's sexy and easy to move to, making it perfect for moms who want to let loose and have some fun. So why not give Dubstep for Moms a try today? You won't be disappointed.
