About Cameron Argon:

Cameron Argon was born in 1990 in Laugana Hills Califronia.

Born and raised in the golden state of California, our subject has always had a passion for music. With a desire to create and innovate, they quickly made a name for themselves in the music scene. Their unique blend of dubstep and death metal has garnered a dedicated following, and their prolific output has solidified their place as a true talent.

But as they say, all good things must come to an end, and our subject eventually found themselves yearning for a change of scenery. So, they packed up their things and made the move to Nevada.

Despite the change in location, our subject's love for music remained as strong as ever. They continued to produce a wide range of styles, from hard-hitting death metal to soul-stirring dubstep. Their passion and personality shine through in every note, making them a truly captivating artist to watch.

So, if you're a fan of music that's bold, original, and full of personality, be sure to keep an eye on this California native-turned-Nevadan. They're sure to have plenty more to offer in the years to come.
