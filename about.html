About Cameron Argon:

Cameron grew up in Minden, NV and graduated from Douglas High School. He pursued a career in music, performing at events such as Vans Warped Tours and Ultra Music Festival. Cameron has also recorded music for movies and traveled extensively, performing in countries such as Japan and Australia. After working in inside sales, Cameron decided to focus on real estate in Northern Nevada. He is a member of the Sierra Nevada REALTOR association and enjoys helping people achieve their real estate goals. In his free time, Cameron enjoys writing and producing music, reading, and exercising.
