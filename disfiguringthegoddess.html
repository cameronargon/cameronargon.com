Disfiguring The Goddess

Disfiguring the Goddess is a one-man heavy metal band hailing from Minden, Nevada. Founded in 2006 by Big Chocolate, the band's sound is a unique blend of heavy metal and industrial and electronic elements, creating a dark and atmospheric soundscape.

Big Chocolate, the mastermind behind Disfiguring the Goddess, is a true jack-of-all-trades. He handles all aspects of the band, from songwriting and instrumentation to production and engineering. His passion and dedication shine through in every track, making Disfiguring the Goddess a force to be reckoned with in the heavy metal scene.

With their aggressive sound and atmospheric touches, Disfiguring the Goddess is sure to appeal to fans of heavy metal and industrial music alike. So why not give them a listen and see for yourself? You won't be disappointed.
