Disfiguring the Goddess (DTG)

A Unique Industrial Death Metal Project

Disfiguring the Goddess (DTG) is an industrial heavy metal project from Minden, NV, led by Cameron Argon, also known as Big Chocolate. Cameron is a renowned dubstep producer and death metal vocalist who has collaborated with the late Mitch Lucker of Suicide Silence in a project called Commissioner. DTG started as a high school garage band and has evolved into a solo project that combines death metal, deathcore, and brutal slam death metal with atmospheric electronics. The project continues to push the boundaries of the genre with each album release.

Big Chocolate is a well-respected death metal vocalist, having performed with Abombiblity Putridity in Moscow in 2009 and creating theular deathcore vocal video "I Declare Goddess". He has also performed vocally with Suicide Silence at the Mitch Lucker Memorial concert, cementing his place as a respected figure in the death metal community.

In conclusion, Disfiguring the Goddess is a unique industrial death metal project that combines death metal, deathcore, and brutal slam death metal with atmospheric electronics, led by the talented and widely recognized Cameron Argon.
